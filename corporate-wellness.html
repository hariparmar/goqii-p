Prepare for the ‘New Normal’

2020 showed us the importance of immunity and its impact on our physical, mental & emotional health, especially among the workforce. Immunity is built through healthy habits such as adequate nutrition, regular exercise, sound sleep and a healthy state of mind, which is now considered the standard norm to live a healthy lifestyle.

wellness

Healthy Workforce = Increased Productivity

Good immunity also positively affects employee productivity, thereby leading to better overall performance. Thus, it is the need of the hour to provide our workforce with health & wellness programs that improve health as well as efficiency at work.

LIFESTYLE TRACK

Bigger organizations with larger workforces tend to have diverse employees w.r.t. age range, medical conditions, fitness goals, lifestyles, cultures, etc. We customize our wellness offerings for a defined group or a selected segment of the workforce within large workforces with collective health goals

REPORT
GENERATION
& ANALYSIS

We profile individuals in an organization and provide them with a health score based on their habits, lifestyle and working conditions. Additionally, we also provide comprehensive information on recommended assessments, setting up reminders, appointments, storing health records, tracking health parameters and conducting lab tests if required.

HEALTH RISK
ASSESSMENT

Health risks due to occupational and lifestyle hazards have gained attention in recent decades, especially in the workplace. To examine your quality of life, we have a tailored set of questions to help us detect your health risks. We provide an HRA score that’ll help you understand your health status.

HEALTH LOCKER

A digital health locker on the GOQii app allows for the storage and transfer of health screening information.
