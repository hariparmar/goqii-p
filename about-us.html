About Us

Founded in 2014, GOQii Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is headquartered in Menlo Park, California with its office in Mumbai, India as well. Since its inception, GOQii has expanded its horizons and transitioned from providing smart health trackers & devices and personal coaching to now establishing an entire ecosystem of preventive healthcare as a viable, long term, mass-market solution.

Our tech-enabled healthcare platform combines state of the art technology and robust data analytics to empower consumers to ‘level up’ their lives by making positive lifestyle changes. Our philosophy of #BeTheForce is well established through our various nationwide initiatives and campaigns that are committed to making our country fit, active and happy.
